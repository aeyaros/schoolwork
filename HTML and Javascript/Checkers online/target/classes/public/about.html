How to play:

In case you were unaware, checkers is a game for two players played on an 8x8 board with alternating red and black squares. Each player has 12 pieces of a specific color (red or black). At the start, pieces are arranged on either side of the board.

Players may move diagonally from square to square. If another player's piece is between your piece and an empty space, you can "capture" the other piece by moving your piece over it. If you can move your piece to a spot on the opposite edge of the board, it becomes a king; king pieces have more freedom of movement and can capture multiple pieces in one move.

When you capture all of the other player's pieces, you win!
